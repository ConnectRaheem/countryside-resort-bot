🍽️ Food & Dining Policy
Room service is available — order via WhatsApp and we will bring food to your room with pleasure. Self-cooking and use of personal cooking appliances in rooms is not permitted. Outside food brought into the resort may not be heated or cooked on resort premises. Our kitchen is open for all your meal needs — just ask us.
🔕 Noise Curfew
All outdoor music, gatherings, and loud activity must end by 11:00 PM. Please be considerate of other guests and our neighbours.
🏊 Swimming Pool
Open 8:00 AM – 8:00 PM daily. Children under 12 must be accompanied by an adult at all times. No diving. Swim at own risk.
🚬 Smoking
Smoking is permitted in designated outdoor areas only. Smoking inside rooms results in a PKR 5,000 cleaning charge.
🚫 Alcohol
Strictly prohibited on all resort premises in accordance with Pakistani law. No exceptions.
👤 Visitors
Non-registered visitors must be approved at the front desk before entry. Day-use fee applies. Unregistered visitors are not permitted in rooms.
🔥 BBQ & Campfires
BBQ only in the designated BBQ area. Do not light fires independently in the orchard, garden, or near the stream.
🛏️ Room Service
Rooms are cleaned daily before noon. Please do not disturb sign will be respected. Check-out: 11:00 AM. Late check-out: PKR 1,500/hr.
💰 Damage Policy
Guests are responsible for any damage to resort property. Replacement or repair cost will be charged. Keys not returned: PKR 500 per key.
🐾 Pets
Pets are not permitted inside rooms. Prior arrangement required if bringing a pet on resort grounds.
🌿 Orchard & Garden
Walk freely and enjoy the orchard. In season, you are welcome to pick fruit. Please do not damage trees, plants, or the stream area.
